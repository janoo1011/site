About Us

THE PIONEER IN IMS TRAINING

We have been building careers and developing Technical skill sets with our world-class training. National Institute of Network Technology is India's most promising hardware and networking training institute, be it professional or personal, computers have become an integral part of our lives. With the advent of IT companies, computers offer an exhaustive list of exciting career opportunities in hardware and networking in the top three slots.

A recent study has revealed that over 1 million jobs are available for expert Hardware and Networking professional both in India and abroad, but there is an acute shortage for value rich Hardware and Networking Professional, to meet this demand NINT has designed and developed. It's curriculum with international business trends with more emphasis on the local industry needs.

OUR TECHNICAL STAFFS AND TRAINING METHODOLOGY

At NINT, we have versatile, highly qualified and certified Engineers with varied academic backgrounds. The teaching staffs are in the field with the complete understanding of the IT IMS industry, its prospects and are provident enough to see to it that you are equipped with the latest knowledge in the ever changing world of IT IMS industry.

After extensive learning, we subject our students to some Real Time situations (which is often created by our staff) and student left alone, to handle the situation, to overcome the problem, with his own solutions. In this way, he is getting experienced, even before the completion of course and is very much ready to seek the job with tremendous confidence

WE BELIEVE IN PRACTICAL LEARNING.

Our labs are enormous and equipped with the latest systems available in the industry today. We also have product maintenance engineers who see to it that our systems are fully functional at all times. We have a good library, shelving books with latest versions and editions covering various topics on Real Time situations and Troubleshooting, etc.
